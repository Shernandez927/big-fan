Who is Trophy Eyes?

Trophy Eyes is an Australian punk rock band from Newcastle, formed in February 2013.
The band consists of vocalist John Floreani, bassist and back-up vocalist Jeremy Winchester, guitarist Andrew Hallett, guitarist Kevin Cross and drummer Blake Caruso.
